Club Description: 
Concordia Music Zone Out (CMZO) is dedicated to provide platforms for Concordia students to express their creative thoughts and perspective through the means of music.
CMZO pledges to adress global and local issues as well as promote cultural engagement among Concordia students.
If you are a creative thinker and want to express yourself through music, contact us as soon as possible for we are here to accomodate all students regardess of their cultural or academic background.
If you love good music and want to meet new people, this is the club to be apart of !
