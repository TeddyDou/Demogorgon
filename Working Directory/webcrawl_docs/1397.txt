Supply Chain & Business Technology Management
The Department of Supply Chain and Business Technology Management at the John Molson School of Business (JMSB), Concordia University strives to equip its students with an integrated set of decision-making skills.
Our department
The department is located on the downtown campus of Concordia University, in beautiful Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Explore our website to find out more about becoming a student here or about exciting career opportunities in our department.
