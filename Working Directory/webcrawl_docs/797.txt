Centraide samosa sale – Revised
Loyola Campus
November 30, 2017, 11:30 a.m. – 2 p.m.
Due to unforeseen circumstances, the Loyola pasta lunch has been cancelled and replaced with a samosa sale on the same day.
2 samosas for $3 or 12 for $15.
Why wait? Pre-order a dozen and pay on the day of the sale by emailing  centraide@concordia.ca  or calling extension 5895.
Where
Loyola Jesuit Hall and Conference Centre (7141 Sherbrooke W.), Loyola Campus
Cost
This event is free
Organizer
centraide@concordia.ca
Organization
Concordia's centraide campaign
