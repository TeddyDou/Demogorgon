Club Description: 
The Concordia Cooking Club wants to empower students by providing the opportunity and tools necessary to discover a passion for cooking while developing a sense of community within Concordia. The Concordia Cooking Club is characterized by the following: teaching healthy cooking habits to its members, facilitating stress relieving activities through interactive cooking classes, utilizing cooking to give back to our community, and creating an atmosphere in which students can build supportive relationships with fellow peers based on the common interest of cooking.
