Health Insurance Q&A Session
November 22, 2017, 2:30 p.m. – 4 p.m.
Are you interested in finding out how to navigate the Quebec Health Care System and how to make use of your International Blue Cross Health Insurance plan? Do you wonder where you can go to see a doctor or how to make a claim for a refund or do you have any specific questions concerning your Blue Cross insurance plan?
If you have answered “yes” to any of this questions, then this session is for you.
Please register in advance for this information session.
