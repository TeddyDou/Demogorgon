OPEN HOUSE
Saturday, February 10, 2018
Your future is open. We can help you shape it.
Spend the day experiencing what it's like to be a Concordia student.
During Open House, you can:
Take a tour: See our campuses, including labs, classrooms, studio spaces and student residences
Attend a presentation, mini-lecture or information session
Chat with current students about their experience
Get valuable feedback as part of Portfolio Day (for future Fine Arts students)
Learn more about academic programs and admissions requirements
Apply for admission on the spot!
