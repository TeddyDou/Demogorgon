Health Services is your on-campus wellness clinic and health promotion center.
The Flu Vaccine Hotline is now open
Health Services is holding flu vaccine clinics beginning November 21st. The Flu Shot Hotline is now OPEN! Call 514-848-2424 ext. 5276 to reserve your dose.
Learn more about the flu clinic and other places in Montreal where you can get vaccinated on the Health Services flu page .
