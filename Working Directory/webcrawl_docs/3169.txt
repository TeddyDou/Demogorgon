Club Description: 
The Concordia Committee for International Relations and Diplomacy (CONCIAD) aims to raise awareness about international affairs and represent the school in related events. One of the goals of the club is to provide a professional and social network for students of interested in international affairs and diplomacy through the organization of social, cultural, and intellectual events annually. We attend conferences such as Model Arab League and Model NATO.
