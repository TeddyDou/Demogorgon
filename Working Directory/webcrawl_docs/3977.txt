About Us
Space Concordia is a student society at Concordia University that throws its members at challenging space and aerospace projects, to yield awesome results. We win Awards at national and international competitions, we showcase our school, but most importantly: Space Concordia teaches and trains excellent young scientists and engineers through hands-on work and an attitude of persistance and collaboration.
