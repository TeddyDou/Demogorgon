Funding and partnership opportunities in France information session (by the Consulate General of France in Montreal)
November 28, 2017, 3 p.m. – 4 p.m.
Prof. Florence Beranger, Scientific and University Cooperation Attaché at the Consulate General of France in Montreal will give us a presentation on funding and partnership opportunities offered by France and how Concordia faculty members and students can take advantage of them. 
Refreshments will be provided.
Please confirm your attendance by November 27th at: studyaway@concordia.ca
You can also join us:
Online from PC, Mac, Linux, iOS or Android at: https://concordia-ca.zoom.us/j/610718930
iPhone one-tap: +16475580588,,610718930#

International numbers available: https://concordia-ca.zoom.us/zoomconference?m=pfgYKHsYvrqY-bv6bYdF0gIpZlwB6X8G
