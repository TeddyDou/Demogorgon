Quebecois Movie Nights
November 24, 2017, 5 p.m. – 8 p.m.
Every last Friday of the month, CSEA and ISO invites you to watch a fun Quebecois movie, eat popcorn and learn more about this beautiful province! The screenings are also followed an informal discussion which everyone is welcome to participate. The goal is to discover local culture and the French language via Quebec's national cinema! 
Whether you are learning French, looking into settling in Quebec after your studies, or simply curious of Quebecois culture, this series is for you!
Movie: TBA
Details:
Room: ISO Workshop room (GM 340)
Duration: 5:00 pm to 8:00 pm
Cost: Free
Popcorn and beverages will be provided!
