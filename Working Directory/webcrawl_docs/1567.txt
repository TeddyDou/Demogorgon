A workout space custom-built to motivate you
You'll find trained and helpful staff present on the floor at all times. We offer a towel service and we are wheelchair accessible. Our state-of-the-art equipment includes 18 treadmills, 19 ellipticals, 10 upright life-cycles, 8 recumbent cycles, 5 Cardio Waves,  5 spinners on the floor (20 in the spinning room), 10 rowers and Technogym Kinesis, a revolutionary new system that allows the user to move three dimensionally to perform more than 500 movements.
