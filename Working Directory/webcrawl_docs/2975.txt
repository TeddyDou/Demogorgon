Aug 17, 2015
kmcloughlin
Language
English
Are you searching for a financial institution to bank with in Montreal?
Here is a list of banks where you may be able to open checking accounts to facilitate your financial activity. Make sure to read a variety of different options to ensure you find the services best for you. Beware of easy credit offers as banks often target students with predatory financial practices. Consult Concordia University's financial mangement page for more information on budgeting and other financial education resources. Education does not have to be a debt sentence.
