Winter Orientation 2018
Kick-start your Concordia life! Orientation isn't just one day – it's a series of events that take place at the beginning of the winter term. Explore the activities below and sign up for as many as you like to maximize your orientation experience. 
All new students
New Student Tours  | Various Dates
Let a member of our Welcome Crew show you around campus.
Hot Cocoa with the Welcome Crew  | January 4 to 12
Stop by one of the Weclome Crew stations for a cup of hot cocoa and get help with any of your beginning-of-term tasks.
Undergraduate orientation activities
Discover Concordia  | January 8 or January 9 (choose one, same content on both days)
Discover a little bit of everything about your new university. Whether you're a native Montrealer or from abroad, this event is for you!
Start Right  | January 11
Get insider tips from our Learning Specialists  and Welcome Crew Mentors about effective study strategies.
MAP to Success  | Various Dates
Map out your personalized action plan to university success in a small-group setting.
Graduate orientation activities
Graduate Student Orientation  | January 9
Connect with representatives from the School of Graduate Studies, discover key resources on campus and network with your fellow graduate students.
Graduate School Base Camp   | January 12 (registration coming soon)
Learn fundamental skills for success in graduate school and beyond with GradProSkills.
International orientation activities
WISE YUL  | Date coming soon
Learn about immigration, health insurance and services available to international students.
Student-led events
Check out orientation and frosh events organized by your student associations.
Browse Student-led Events - COMING SOON
More welcome events
Units from across the university are welcoming new students and parents.
