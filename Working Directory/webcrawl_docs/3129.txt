List of student groups
Explore Concordia's countless student groups and get involved in University life! Concordia's rich collection of student groups include cultural, religious, social and academic groups.
How to use the list
Scroll through the list below to see all current student groups. Make sure to check out the groups often as this list is updated frequently throughout the year. Don’t see what you’re looking for? Find out how to start a student group or contact David Baker from the Dean of Students david.baker@concordia.ca , (514) 848-2424, ext. 3517.
