Concordia Alumni Holiday Reception-Boston
It's time for us to mingle and jingle
November 30, 2017, 6 p.m. – 8 p.m.
Ring in the holiday season with your fellow Boston-area alumni and Temi Akin-Aina, associate director of Concordia's  Alumni Relations .
Thursday, November 30, 2017
6 – 8 p.m.
The Liberty Hotel – Rotunda 4th floor  215 Charles St. Boston, Mass.
$10
Plus-ones welcome and encouraged.
RSVP by November 27
Information:  AlumniEvents@Concordia.ca

The Liberty Hotel — Rotunda 4th floor
215 Charles St. Boston, Mass.
