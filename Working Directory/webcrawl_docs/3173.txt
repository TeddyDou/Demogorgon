Club Description: 
Concordia Cycling is an association regrouping all the undergraduate cycling enthusiast of Concordia. The main goal of the club is to provide a safe and friendly environment for weekly organized rides opened to all.
Rides are held in the greater Montreal area. Throughout the winter season, spinning session take place every week at LeGym. All undergraduate students are welcome to become a member of Concordia Cycling. The membership will give you the opportunity to participate to all organized rides and spinning sessions. Being a member will also give you different opportunities our sponsor will provide us.
As mentioned, one of the main goal of Concordia Cycling is to provide a friendly environment to the members. All rides will be strictly non-competitive, meaning that we will start together and finish together. No experience is needed, all we require is your passion for cycling.
All information related to the rides and spinning sessions will be posted in the Calendar section of this website and all communication in the Forum section and on our Facebook page Concordia Cycling .
