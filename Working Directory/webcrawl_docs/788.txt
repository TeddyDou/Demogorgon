Free Rapid-HIV testing TODAY!
Regular sexually transmitted infection testing is an important part of taking care of your health
November 29, 2017 – November 30, 2017, 12 p.m. – 4 p.m.
Many Concordians are not aware of their HIV status. Knowing your status can help you get and stay healthy.
Come by the Concordia Student Union (CSU) H-711 Wednesday and Thursday, 12-4 to get your free Rapid-HIV test: results in 20 minutes. Confidential. Performed by Health Services nurses.
For more information about HIV, check out CATIE's HIV Basics website.
Where
Room 711, Henry F. Hall Building (1455 De Maisonneuve W.), Sir George Williams Campus
Cost
free
