JMSB LIFE Orientation Fall 2016
JMSB LIFE (Living the International Faculty Experience) is an initiative intended to enhance the experience of international students at the John Molson School of Business. Through a series of workshops, social and cultural events that complement the activities of Concordia’s International Students Office , JMSB LIFE engages international students and their parents from the moment they arrive and provides them with a sense of community that helps them overcome issues like language barriers and culture shock.
Who is it for?
International students (undergraduate and graduate)
JMSB LIFE offers something for every international student. Those who participate:
Make new friends
Feel empowered
Increase their chances for academic success
Are given the chance to embrace Canadian culture
Have the opportunity to connect potential future employers
Parents of international students
This initiative provides parents with reassurance, allows them to meet other parents of international students and gives them a better understanding of the Canadian university experience enabling them to:
Better assist their child
Know what questions to ask to best support their child
Be more involved in their child’s education 
Local students
JMSB LIFE’s vast network of volunteers gives local students the chance to:
Engage in community service
Make cross-cultural connections
Learn a language
Develop their leadership and mentorship skills
Work closely with senior administration
