Bob Black
Chief Project Development Officer, Edmonton Oilers
BOB BLACK, Chief Project Development Officer of the Edmonton Oilers, is coming to #JMSM17 !
Bob was born in Yellowknife, Northwest Territories and graduated from the University of Alberta’s faculty of law. He was a senior partner at Brownlee LLP. a respected Calgary law firm for 25 years.
In 2009, he undertook the position of Executive Vice-President of Rexall Sports & Entertainment for the Katz Group. He was subsequently in charge of designing and developing Rogers Place as Vice President of the Edmonton Arena Corp and Katz Group. He currently serves on the One Edmonton Committee and the Downtown Vibrancy Task Force.
