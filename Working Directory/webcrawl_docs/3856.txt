MIKE KELLY, Hockey Analyst for  SPORTLOGiQ , is joining us for  #JMSM17 !
Mike Kelly currently oversees media partnerships at SportLogIQ. Apart from his work at SportLogIQ, he also does statistical analysis for the NHL network as an analyst for TSN 690. Mike has also been heavily involved on the business side of analytics by consulting in player development with NHL and Minor hockey players and providing statistical analysis on contract negotiations with NHLPA agents.
