Club Description: 
The Concordia Esports Association (CESA) is looking to gather all gaming enthusiasts, casual or competitive, alike. CESA organizes competitions, LANs and get-togethers to let all students show their love for gaming. The club offers students a first step into the world of Esports. By doing so, we hope to get passionate students engaged with their community through video games.
