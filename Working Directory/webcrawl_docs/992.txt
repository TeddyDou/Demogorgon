Major League Sports Teams and Municipal Politics
Ask our Experts
Posted on October 19, 2017
With the re-election of Calgary Mayor, Naheed Nenshi reigniting conversations about "stadium politics" in Canada, what do the rumours of reviving the Montreal Expos mean for Montreal during this municipal election?    
An expert in sports economics, Moshe Lander is prepared to discuss the economic impacts of hosting a Major League Baseball team in Montreal.
