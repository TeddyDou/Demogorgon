Arts, Culture & Technology
Milieux Institute for Arts, Culture and Technology at Concordia launched March 30. It is an ambitious institute for research-creation working at the intersection of  design, art, culture and technology. Above all, it is a site of innovation, where thinking and making come together to transform our encounter with technology.
