Ross Mahoney
Ross Mahoney
Assitant General Manager, Washington Capitals
The Assistant General Manager of the  Washington Capitals , ROSS MAHONEY is coming to Montreal for the  22nd Annual John Molson Sports Business Conference !
Ross has been the assistant general manager for the organization for the last 3 seasons. He plays a key role in the team’s asset management, developmental programs, and amateur scouting.
Before working for the Capitals, Mahoney worked with the Buffalo Sabres and Vancouver Canucks. Mahoney was inducted in the Saskatchewan Sports Hall of Fame in 1999. He attended the University of Regina and spent 17 years as a teacher.
We’re excited to have Mahoney as a speaker for  #JMSM17 !
