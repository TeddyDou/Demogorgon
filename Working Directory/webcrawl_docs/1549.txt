Frontline Administrative Service Team (F.A.S.T)
We want your experience at Concordia to be successful and the start of the academic year is always an exciting and busy time for new and returning Concordia students. You may have such questions as: How can I pay my tuition? Where can I obtain my student ID card? How do I request a Permanent Code?
Throughout the year, you can get answers to these questions – and many more – at the Birks Student Service Centre  in the  McConnell Library Building , room LB-185 .
At the beginning of the Fall semester, services will be enhanced at various times on weekdays from:
Monday, August 28, 2017 to Friday, September 22, 2017
We look forward to seeing you on campus!
Enhanced service points at the start of the term
Additional Service Assistants will be available to:
Respond to inquiries about
Tuition and fees
Account restrictions (registration blocks)
Permanent code surcharges
payment and withdrawal deadlines
late payment and interest fees
payment arrangements
Accept and process payments  made by personal cheque, certified cheque, money order and debit (Interac) card;
Accept and verify Permanent Code  documents; and remove a Permanent Code surcharge from your student account if the documents are in order;
Accept and verify proof of Quebec Residency Code documents for students who were born in Quebec; and change tuition fee assessment to Quebec Residency rate;
Negotiate payment arrangements for students with an outstanding balance from a previous term in order to remove an accounts restriction.
Where:
Birks Student Service Centre , LB 185 .
When:
Services at the general service points will be available from:
August 28, 2017 to September 22, 2017
Monday to Wednesday:
9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Thursday:
9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Friday:
9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Student I.D. card service points
Undergraduate and Graduate students will be able to obtain their Concordia student I.D. card* at the student I.D. card service points.
Where:
Student I.D. card service points will be located in the cage area at the back of the McConnell Library Building Atrium .
When:
The student I.D. card service points will be available from:
Monday, August 28, 2017
11 a.m. to 5 p.m.
August 29, 2017 to September 22, 2017
Monday to Friday: 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
*Please note: You must bring one piece of photo ID as well as your student ID number. Student cards can only be produced once you have registered for courses (48 hours prior.)
