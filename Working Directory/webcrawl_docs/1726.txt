About us
Under the leadership of the vice-president, Advancement and External Relations, Advancement and Alumni Relations friend-raises and fundraises for the university.
Advancement and Alumni Relations (AAR) is led by Bram Freedman, vice-president, Advancement and External Relations. He is is also president of the Concordia University Foundation . 
AAR also organizes events for alumni, students, donors and friends and builds relationships with the community.
AAR publishes the monthly Accent newsletter and Concordia University Magazine in winter, spring and fall for more than 200,000 alumni and friends.
AAR collaborates with the Concordia University Alumni Association to offer alumni and students a rich and varied selection of benefits and services as well as social, educational and cultural events, including Homecoming, class reunions and networking opportunities.
Information collected via our website or email is subject to our privacy policy .

Our mission
We are committed to good governance and best practices as we anticipate the needs of our Concordia partners in order to advance the university’s core academic, research and community engagement missions.
Our vision
To establish ourselves as leaders in our specific areas of expertise so as to safeguard and enhance Concordia’s reputation and place in Montreal, Quebec, Canada and the world.  
Concordia Foundation
The Concordia University Foundation plays an important role in ensuring the long-term viability of Concordia’s numerous programs and initiatives.

Concordia University Hong Kong Foundation
