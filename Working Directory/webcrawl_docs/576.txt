Prizes & Medals
At each convocation ceremony, a number of distinguished graduates are honoured by receiving special awards and prizes. 
Competitions that fall under the Doctoral Dissertation category are administered by the School of Graduate Studies and details on internal deadlines, application guidelines and forms, and links to agency sites are provided under the respective section. 
Graduating prizes are administered by specific Departments and Faculties, and students should contact the respective units accordingly.
