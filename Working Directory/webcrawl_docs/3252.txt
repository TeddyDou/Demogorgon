Club Description: 
The purpose of the Shidokan Kendo & Iaido Club is to teach Japanese swordsmanship, its physical techniques and spirit, through the practice of Kendo and Iaido. Both Japanese martial arts will help develop one's sense of spirit, confidence, as well as physical and mental strength. "Be better today than you were yesterday, and then strive to become better still."
