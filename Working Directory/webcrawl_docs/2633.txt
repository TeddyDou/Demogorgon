Urban Futures: Developing Conscientious Competition Proposals
DART498 Guest Speaker Series – Summer 2017
Reusing Materials from the Built Environment and the Architect’s Point of View
Marta Masferrer Juliol, Architect and Urban Designer, Lecturer UQAM – June 6, 2017
Architect and urban designer Marta Masferrer Juliol speaks about the recycling and reuse of construction materials in architectural and public art projects.
DART498 was the summer incubator course for the Legado Public Art Competition offered by the department of Design and Computation Arts, in association with the Institute for Urban Future’s City Bureau. A series of practitioners and experts were invited to speak in class on various aspects of developing public art competition proposals. The course was taught by Moana Andraos and Melissa Mongiat.
