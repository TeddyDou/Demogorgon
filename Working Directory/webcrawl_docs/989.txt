Fall/Winter Registration
Refer to the  Graduate Registration Guide  or consult your  Graduate Program Director  for exact dates of registration and for academic advice.
September 2017
Fri, Sep. 1
Last day for final electronic submission of thesis through  Spectrum , the Concordia Repository, for students who are graduating in the Fall Term
Mon, Sep. 4
Labour Day, University closed
Tue, Sep. 5
Classes begin, Fall Term
Tue, Sep. 5
Late registration and course change period begins
Students incur a late registration fee when they register on or after the date that classes officially begin across the University
Mon, Sep. 18
Deadline for withdrawal with tuition refund * from two-term (/3 DNE) and Fall-term courses (/2 DNE)
Mon, Sep. 18
Last day to add two-term (/3) and Fall-term (/2) courses
Mon, Sep. 18
Deadline for withdrawal from program for Fall Session
Mon, Sep. 18
In Progress deadline to submit coursework to professors for Summer 2017 courses
October 2017
Sun, Oct. 1
Last day to apply for re-evaluation of courses ending in August 2017 (/1)
Mon, Oct. 9
Thanksgiving Day, University closed (see December 5, 2017)
November 2017
Mon, Nov. 6
Last day for academic withdrawal from Fall-term courses (/2 DISC)
Mon, Nov. 6
Last day for Master's and PhD students to withdraw from their program with /3 courses
Students must DISC any /3 courses by this date, and withdraw from their program by the winter DNE deadline (/4 DNE) to avoid being charged for the winter term
Mon, Nov. 20
Fall Convocations

Fri, Dec. 1
Last day to apply for Quebec Resident status for Fall Term 2017

Last day of classes, Fall Term

Make-up day for classes scheduled on Monday, October 9
Wed, Dec. 6
Examinations begin
Wed, Dec. 20
Examinations end

to Wed, Jan. 3
Holiday period, University closed

Mon, Jan. 8
Classes begin, Winter Term

Mon, Jan. 15
Last day for students to apply for Spring graduation

Deadline for withdrawal with tuition refund * from Winter-term courses (/4 DNE)
Mon, Jan. 22
Deadline for withdrawal from program for Winter Session
Mon, Jan. 22
Last day to add Winter-term courses (/4)
Mon, Jan. 22
In Progress deadline to submit coursework to professors for Fall 2017 courses

Thu, Feb. 1
Last day to apply for re-evaluation of courses ending in December 2017 (/2)

Concordia President's Holiday, University closed
Sun, Feb. 25
Mid-term break ends

Mon, Mar. 19
Last day for academic withdrawal from two-term (/3 DISC) Winter-term (/4 DISC) courses

University closed (see April 17, 2018)

University closed (see April 17, 2018)

Sun, Apr. 1
University closed

University closed
