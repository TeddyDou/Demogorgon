Multi-faith food drive – donations accepted until April 27!
Collect and donate non-perishable items for the NDG Food Depot
October 6, 2017 – April 27, 2018, 11:30 a.m. – 1 p.m.
To embrace our city and neighborhood, Concordians are encouraged to collect non-perishable food items for donation to the NDG Food Depot.
Drop off your generous food items in the SP Atrium at Loyola Campus every first and third Friday of each month from 12 to 1 p.m.!
Where
Room SP Atrium, Richard J. Renaud Science Complex (7141 Sherbrooke W.), Loyola Campus
Cost
This event is free
Speakers
Father Paul Anyidoho
