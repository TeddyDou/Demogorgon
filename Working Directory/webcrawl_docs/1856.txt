Beyond Disciplines: The podcast
A podcast series designed to encourage discussion that spans the disciplines of Concordia University's Arts and Science Faculty.
Mix it up, experiment boldly, and go beyond disciplines! An ongoing series of public events and podcasts designed to encourage discussion that spans the disciplines of Concordia’s Faculty of Arts and Science. 
These podcasts examine major questions of the day, from a variety of viewpoints.
