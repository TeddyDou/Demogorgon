School of Graduate Studies
INTERDISCIPLINARY PROGRAMS
Interdisciplinary collaboration and research is what gives Concordia its edge. 
Pursue your own research interests in a wide range of areas, from sciences to the Humanities. Interdisciplinary programs allow you to design your program and explore your passions in a setting that provides enough structure to stay on track.
Individualized Program
Navigate new terrains of scholarship in areas not covered by existing graduate programs.
PhD in Humanities
Transform knowledge and creative expression through interdisciplinary research and research-creation.
Graduate Certificate in University Teaching
Gain a competitive edge with in-depth knowledge and skills related to university teaching.
Concordia University Interdisciplinary Summer Institute (CUISI)
Get hands-on experiences in areas as diverse as aerospace, journalism, nutrition, oral history and more.
