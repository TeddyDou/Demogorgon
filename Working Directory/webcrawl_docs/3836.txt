Event description
Description
JHMA is excited to invite you this year's HRM WEEK 2017!!
HRM Week is a series of events for students who are curious about the HR and Management major. All JMSB students are welcome to attend! We have some great things planned over these next three days so be sure to buy your tickets as spots are limited.
Details pertaining to each event:
**DAY 1**
HR WORKSHOP - Wednesday, November 22nd, 2017
Time: 6:00pm-9:00pm
Location: MB. S1.105
Dress Code: Business Casual
Price: $5
**DAY 2**
MANAGEMENT DAY- Thursday, November 23rd, 2017
Time: 6:00pm-9:00pm
Location: MB. Conference 9th floor
Dress Code: Business Casual
Price: $5
**DAY 3**
HR/MANA CASE COMPETITION- Friday, November 24th, 2017
Time: 9am-5pm
Location: MB. Conference 9th floor
Dress Code: Business Professional
Price: $20
CASE COMPETITION INFO:
- 8 teams in total
- teams of 4 students are required
- if you have a team of 4, email your names to info@jhma.ca AS SOON AS YOU SIGN UP
- if you don't have a team, email your name, we'll assign you a group and we'll email your team members' names by NOVEMBER 20th
EARLY BIRD CASE COMPETITION TICKETS: $15 --- ON SALE: NOVEMBER 3rd 2017 at NOON
REGULAR ADMISSION TICKETS --- ON SALE: NOVEMBER 6th 2017 at NOON
Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity, we look forward to seeing you there!!!
