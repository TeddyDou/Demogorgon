Club Description: 
As we value cooperation, integration and ethics, our resources and events are geared towards cultivating a sense of community and to provide an environment to build long-lasting friendships.
We also seek to actively expand our professional network by fostering strong bonds with the inner and outer societies surrounding campus.
When you become a member of CCAS, you get fabulous benefits such as discounts from our generous sponsors, priority access to our exclusive events, and an opportunity to hang out with our passionate crew, and make new friends!
