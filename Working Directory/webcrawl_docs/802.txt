Hauntings
November 30, 2017 – December 1, 2016, 7:30 p.m. – 10:30 p.m.
Hauntings: a boutiqueARCADE, is a site-specific theatre creation that takes the form of a “pop-up” retail outlet that holds a special collection of shoes and shoeboxes containing miniature scenes and fictional realities inspired by the Montreal neighbourhood of St. Henri’s changing cultural landscape.
Creators: Avery Burrow, Marlon Campellone, Emma De Lafontaine-Jovani, Marie-Michel Durand, Justine Feuiltault, Olivia Jaen Flores, Sarah Iorio, Madison Jolliffe, Kait Landry, and Jenn Townsend
Curated by: Shauna Janssen
*Poster Image created by Concordia Theatre Alumnus Simon Pelletier assisted by Stephanie Lebel
