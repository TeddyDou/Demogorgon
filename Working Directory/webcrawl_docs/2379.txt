Technical Centre
The Science Technical Centre is located in the Richard J. Renaud Science Pavilion on the Loyola campus and provides mechanical and electronics shop support, as well as glassblowing services, for teaching and research laboratories in the Faculty of Arts and Science.
Drop-in or contact us at the numbers listed below to discuss your service and project needs.
Mechanical shop
Location:  SP-S180.9
Mechanical Technician:  Richard Allix
Electronic shop
Location:  SP-S180.3
Electronics Technicians:  Gheorghe Dan Duru ,  Robert Pisarsky
Glassblowing facility
Location:  SP-S180.27
Glassblower: Vacant
