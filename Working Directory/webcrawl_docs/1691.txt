ALUMNI MATTERS: GRADUATION CONFERENCE
A yearly conference to help transition students into their professional lives
March 8 & 9, 2017
These two days of activities will to help you transition from school into your professional life!
Get inspired, learn practical skills — like negotiating a job offer and how to deliver a knockout presentation — and meet successful alumni offering career advice in a variety of fields.
Open to graduating students and recent grads.
Wednesday, March 8, 2017
10:30 – 11:15 a.m., EV 1.615: Workshop
Tips and strategies on giving presentations in the workplace
12 – 3 p.m., MB-6.101, 1450 Guy St.
Get your LinkedIn profile picture taken and pick up some alumni business cards
12–12:45 p.m., FB 520: Alumni roundtable
Mergers and acquisitions with Zeno Santache, BComm 83, Chief Financial Officer, Claridge
12 – 1 p.m., MB 5.255: Panel discussion
Lessons from abroad - the impact of studying, working and living abroad
1:30–2:30 p.m., MB 5.255: Panel discussion
To be or not to be — a grad student - why and how to apply to grad school
2 – 2:45 p.m., FB-520: Alumni roundtable
Financial planning with Christos Retsinas, BComm 06, Director of Business Development at Freedom 55 Financial
3 p.m. - 4 p.m., MB 5.215
Invest in yourself: The necessity of investment and insurance
Strategies for investments and insuring yourself wisely, with our alumni affinity partners
4:15 p.m. - 5 p.m., FB 520: Alumni roundtable
Engineering project management with Jignesh Patel, BEng 15, Project Manager at Ben Crudo Consulting Inc.
6 – 7 p.m., MB 6th floor: Keynote address
A quick change, and slow transition into… what’s next?
Keynote speaker: Helen Downie, BA 11, MA 16, change management lead at Concordia
7 – 8:30 p.m., MB 6th floor
Networking cocktail
Thursday, March 9, 2017
12 – 3 p.m., LB-362, Friends of the Library Room, 1400 De Maisonneuve St. W.
Get your LinkedIn profile picture taken and pick up some alumni business cards
11:15 a.m. – 12p.m., LB 361: Alumni roundtable
Real estate development with Randall Ettinger, BA 06, Co-Founder of Perspective Group
12:30 – 1:30 p.m., LB 361: Workshop
School's Not Out - The importance of Continuing Education
What is continuing education and how can it help your career?
12:30–1:15 p.m., LB 362: Alumni roundtable
National security with Stephen Covey, BA 94, MA 00, Chief Security Officer and Chief of Police for Canadian National Railway (CN)
2–2:45 p.m., LB 361: Alumni roundtable
Creative arts with Scott MacLeod, BFA 03, MA 13, painter, photographer and filmmaker
3 – 4 p.m., LB 361: Presentation by Career Management Services
Negotiating the job offer : tips and strategies to use at various stages of negotiations
3 – 4 p.m., LB 362: Alumni roundtable
Music industry brand management with Giuliana Bevilacqua, music marketing specialist and Co-op student in Marketing and Management
5:30–6:30 p.m., LB 361: Workshop
Learn how to use the power of social media to brand yourself and grow your network, with media consultant Christina Paylan, BA 05, MBA 16.
