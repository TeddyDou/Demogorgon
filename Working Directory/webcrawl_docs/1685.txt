Other Concordia resources
Career and Planning Services (CAPS)
Alumni are entitled to use the services offered by Concordia's Career and Planning Services (CAPS), many of which are free of charge. 
Visit CAPS
Career Management Services (CMS)
John Molson School of Business alumni can access the CMS job posting site, register for career events, browse online career material and use advising services.
Visit CMS
Institute for Co-operative Education (Co-op)
Students gain valuable work experience related to their field of study and earn a salary through Co-op work-study placements.
