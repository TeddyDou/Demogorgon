Published on Nov 2, 2017
The Azrieli Institute of Israel Studies organized a seminar on September 27, 2016. Dr. Bina Freiwald (Professor in the Department of English & Director of the Humanities PhD Program) presented on "Ambivalence: Representations of Israel in Contemporary Jewish Canadian Writing" and Dr. Csaba Nikolenyi (Professor in the Department of Political Science and the Director of the Azrieli Institute of Israel Studies) presented on ""Avrasha, come home!" The stinking trick" and the fall of the Israel's National Unity government in 1990."
