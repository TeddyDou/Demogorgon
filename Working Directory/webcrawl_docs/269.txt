We're the place to enhance your student experience at Concordia! Whether you are looking for help with a student group or whether you want to get involved in Concordia’s rich life outside the classroom, we are here to support you.
Featured news
The Sustainable Event Guide is Live!
Be a leader in sustainability and use Concordia's Sustainable Event Guide when planning your event! Made by both students and Concordia staff, the guide is full of useful information, how to’s, tips, and cost saving measures. It has also been geared specifically to our university in the hopes of helping make events more streamlined.
Check it out and make sure your event is not only successful, but sustainable too!

Student ID Cards at Loyola
The Dean of Students Office now prints ID cards for students at our Loyola Campus office (AD-121). The service is available during the following times:
Monday to Friday  9:45 am – 11:45 am and 1:45 pm – 4:00 pm
