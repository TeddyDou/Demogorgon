Concordia Student Union - Special Clubs Funding
Special Clubs Funding is available for CSU Clubs who wish to fund a new initiative not included in their approved annual budget.
All funding is provided to projects in the form of reimbursement for expenses. Approved projects will be required to submit original receipts for all expenditures.
Please contact the CSU VP Clubs & Internal (clubs@csu.qc.ca) or the CSU Clubs Director (clubsdirector@csu.qc.ca) should you have any questions regarding your application.
