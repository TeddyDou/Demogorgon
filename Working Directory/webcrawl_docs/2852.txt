Flickr Photo Editor
The new Flickr photo editor fulfills a great need for online photo editing after the discontinuation of the online Aviary photo editing facilitation.
However, there are a few critical requirements that need to be urgently considered for improving the utility of this new tool:
*Provision of a cropping grid with standard formats such as one-thirds, center-lines.
*Provision of a perspective control tool or straightening tool.
*Facilitation for scaling down images to standard display sizes such as 1024x, 2048x.
*A watermarking tool.
