Club Description: 
The Concordia University Pagan Society (CUPS) promotes and accepts Paganism of all kinds.
CUPS has been a Concordia University club since 1994, with the mandate being to provide opportunities for education, gathering, and worship to Pagans at Concordia and the students of surrounding Quebec communities, to provide these individuals with such resources as the club may have available, and to promote public education and discussion on Paganism and related topics.
Please request to join the Facebook group if you'd like to be a member!
