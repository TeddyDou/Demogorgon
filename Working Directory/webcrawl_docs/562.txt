Le Gym
Le Gym is a comprehensive fitness facility located on the downtown campus. It has the latest exercise equipment, experienced personal trainers, and many classes, covering aerobics, fitness, martial arts and more.

Intramurals
Campus Recreation offers a variety of sports leagues  on the Loyola Campus at the Ed Meagher Arena, Concordia Gym, Concordia Stadium and the Stinger Dome.
 
Rentals & facilities
Fitness and sports facilities can be found on both campuses. Loyola's athletics complex is the focal point for intramural leagues. The Sir George Williams campus is home to Le Gym , a comprehensive workout facility. Some venues are also available to rent .
Please consult the facilities schedules to find open time for drop-in activities.
Sports camps
Nobody loves sports more than the athletes and coaches from the Concordia Stingers . They share their commitment to healthy living with children of all ages at sports camps on the Loyola Campus.
See upcoming camps
PERFORM Conditioning Floor
On the Loyola Campus, the  PERFORM Conditioning Floor  is the place for Concordia students to work out. It offers a large variety of equipment to train on, innovative individual conditioning programs, a healthy weight program, and small group training.
