Exam Strategies for Business, Engineering or Computer Science Students
November 23, 2017, 10:15 a.m. – 12 p.m.
Succeeding in an exam depends on how well you prepare for it as much as it depends on how well you perform on the actual exam. Learn tips on how to do better in your problem-solving exams.
This workshop is also offered on the following date:
October 2
