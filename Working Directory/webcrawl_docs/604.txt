Monthly permit parking (Faculty & Staff Only)
Permits (Monthly Rates)
Monthly Parking Permits are available for the following spaces:
Behind Hall Building and Annexes : $142/month
Indoors Samuel Bronfman Building : $137/month
1. A parking agreement must be signed and respected when owning a parking permit.
2. Monthly payments can be made through Automatic Payroll Deduction.
For information on parking permits, locations and availability; email parking@concordia.ca
Note: All permit spaces are complete, if you would like to add your name to the waitlist for the next available space, go to myconcordia. 
 
Rates include GST and PST.  Rates are subject to change.
