Explore Concordia page
Education
PhD (University of Rochester)
Fields of specialization
Applied macroeconomics, Labor market dynamics, Empirical finance, Quantitative theory.
Research interests
Multi-sector models, mobility, impacts of sectoral shocks on labour and financial markets, simulation-based estimation.
Professional experience
Dr. Lkhagvasuren joined the Department of Economics of Concordia University in July 2007.  From July 2006 through June 2007, he worked as Senior Research Associate at Research Computing of Finance department of Kellogg School of Management.
