Welcome to
The Conseil des arts de Montreal
The Conseil identifies, supports and recognizes artistic excellence. Through its subsidy programs, operating grants, residencies, mobilization activities and awards—including the Grand Prix du Conseil des arts de Montréal—the Conseil is the proud partner of over 400 Montreal cultural organizations and collectives.
By helping to present the works of artists in all areas of the island, the Conseil has made Montreal the vibrant and creative city it is today.
