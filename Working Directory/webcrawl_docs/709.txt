Creative expression drop-in sessions
Every Thursday afternoon at SARC this fall!
September 14, 2017 – December 21, 2017, 2 p.m. – 4 p.m.
Have you been looking for support, but aren't sure where to start?
Come join us every Thursday for an afternoon of creative expression. Colouring, relaxation and refreshments are the only thing on the agenda.
Feel free to bring a friend!
Where
Room H-645, Henry F. Hall Building (1455 De Maisonneuve W.), Sir George Williams Campus
Cost
This event is free
Organizer
Ashley Allen
