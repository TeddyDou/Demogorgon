Club Description: 
The Management Consulting Club (MCC) strives to develop and strengthen the relationships between undergraduate and graduate students of Concordia University and consulting firms. We aim to educate students about various opportunities in the consulting field through information sessions and networking events.
Our mandate is to attract, retain and prepare talented and passionate Concordians to pursue career in Consulting, by offering them all the tools, training-sessions and real-life situations.
