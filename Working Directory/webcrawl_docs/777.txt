JMSB MSc Graduate Programs Information Session
Master of Science (MSc) in Finance, Management or Marketing Information Session
November 27, 2017, 6 p.m. – 7:30 p.m.
This information session will cover all aspects of the MSc  (Finance, Marketing and Management) programs including an overview of the programs, information on the application process, admission requirements, curriculum and extra-curricular opportunities, plus insight into the overall learning experience.
Register now!
Where
Room MB 10.302, John Molson Building (1450 Guy), Sir George William Campus
Cost
This event is free
Organizer
Ayanna Durant
Organization
Graduate Student Recruitment Office, John Molson School of Business
