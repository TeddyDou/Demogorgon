The John Molson School of Business provides an engaging learning and research environment that inspires us to go beyond the commonplace for the development of business and society.

AACSB accreditation
AACSB International accreditation represents the highest standard of achievement for business schools, worldwide.

Beta Gamma Sigma
Each year, a group of our top business students are elected to lifetime membership into the Beta Gamma Sigma honour society in recognition of their outstanding academic achievements. More about Beta Gamma Sigma .

The John Molson School of Business Building was granted Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) Silver certification status by the Canada Green Building Council.
