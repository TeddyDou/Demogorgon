Club Description: 
EMAC is a student run group at Concordia that serves as a platform to present audio/visual and oth performances ad installations. Our mission is to promote cross disciplinary / departmental collaborations. EMAC'S objective are achieved trough events of various nature, from perfomance shows, discussion pannels, and workshops.
