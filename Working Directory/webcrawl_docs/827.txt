5:30 p.m. – 7:30 p.m.
Wild Talks: Michael Rakowitz
Michael Rakowitz is an Iraqi-Americam artist living and working in Chicago who operates within art spaces and beyond them. In this public lecture, Rakowitz will discuss (g)hosting, a term he uses to explore the intersection of hospitality and hostility in his work, as well as the recuperation of disappeared objects, smells, tastes, customs and relationships through reactivations and surrogates.
When
November 21, 2017, 5:30 p.m. – 7:30 p.m.
Where
Sir George Williams Campus H - Henry F. Hall Building (1455 De Maisonneuve W.)
