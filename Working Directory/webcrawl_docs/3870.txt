Becky Magaw
Director of Human Resources, Columbus Blue Jackets 
BECKY MAGAW, Director of Human Resources for the  Columbus Blue Jackets  is joining us for #JMSM17 !
Becky earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Health & Sports Studies and a minor in Organizational Management from Miami University in Oxford, Ohio, in 2004. She went on to earn her Master of Arts in Sport Management from The Ohio State University in Columbus, Ohio, in 2005. She began her career in 2006 with TrueSports, Inc., owner and operator of Mid-Ohio Sports Car Course and The Mid-Ohio School, as Media & Communications Coordinator.
In 2008, she joined the National Hockey League’s Columbus Blue Jackets as a Partnership Account Specialist and in 2011, she became Partnership Activation Manager. In 2014, she was named lead Project Manager for the 2015 NHL All-Star Game. Following All-Star, she transitioned to lead the Human Resources department with the Blue Jackets, while concurrently studying for a second Master’s degree in Human Resources & Employment Relations from Penn State University.
Originally from Wyoming, Ontario, Canada, Becky and her husband Troy have two children, Reese (4) and Graeme (2).
We look forward to having her here!
