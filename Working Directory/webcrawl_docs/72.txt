Social media Do's and Don'ts
Read full article
DO
post beautiful photos of Montreal, Concordia or your dog.
DO engage in conversations.
DO set your privacy settings beyond the default.
DO celebrate amazing moments in your life.
DO give constructive criticism.
DO help your peers share their achievements.
Don’t post photos or videos of yourself engaging in questionable activities. Don’t react emotionally during conversations and never resort to bullying, humiliation or threats. Don’t trash your teachers, organizations, potential employers or anyone else for that matter. Don’t lie, cheat or plagiarize. Don’t think your retweets or likes aren’t representing you. Don’t use expletives in rants about how much you hate something. Don’t rely on privacy settings entirely and never share sensitive personal information.
