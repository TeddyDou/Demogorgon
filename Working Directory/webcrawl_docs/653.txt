Network maintenance: Nov 02
Posted on October 31, 2017
Maintenance affecting Concordia's network and VPN services is scheduled to be performed on November 02, 2017 from 6:30 a.m. until 7:00 a.m.
During the maintenance window, users will lose internet connection intermittently and the VPN service will be unavailable for the duration of the maintenance.
If you have questions about the maintenance period or service interruption, contact the Service Desk by email at help@concordia.ca or call 514-848-2424, ext. 7613 to speak with a Service Desk agent between 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. on weekdays.
