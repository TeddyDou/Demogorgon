Funding and support
The Faculty of Fine Arts offers a full range of research services through the  Office of the Associate Dean Research , working in conjunction with Concordia's central  Office of Research  (OOR) to support the development and growth of creative research and scholarship in fine arts at Concordia University.
Information for faculty & staff
Faculty and staff can find information about research funding on Cspace (login required):

Funding opportunities for graduate students
A number of awards are available to graduate students. Visit the Graduate Studies web site to make an advanced search of applicable funding. An example of international scholarships that have benefitted Fine Arts students and graduates is the Brucebo Scholarship for study and travel in Sweden.
