CSEP Information Session
Come find out about how you can go study abroad!
November 22, 2017, 3 p.m. – 4 p.m.
Our regular information sessions on the Concordia Student Exchange Program are the first step to studying abroad at one of our 174 partner schools in 39 countries worldwide, all the while earning credits that can go towards your degree back home! Drop in on our CSEP info session to find out more about this amazing opportunity.
