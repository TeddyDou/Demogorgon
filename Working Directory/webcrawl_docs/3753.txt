Starting Up
JMMA and Enactus are introducing Starting Up as a gathering of entrepreneurial minds. A place where the knowledge and tools from successful start ups will be passed on to you so you can commence your journey as an entrepreneur.
Information
Name: Starting Up Date: November 3rd, 2015 Time: 6pm-9pm + Networking Where: JMSB, MB 2.130 Cost: $10 Includes: Snacks, beverages, networking, entrepreneurial tools
Want to know more?
If you're someone who has an idea, and the reoccurring dream of starting your own business, this is the place to start. This event is to get to you to take action; to start thinking about how to start, and to learn how to make your dreams a reality. You will be part of an interactive panel, a speaker series and a networking cocktail with entrepreneurs that were in your shoes not too long ago. Attend an evening full of tips, inspirational stories, how-to's, and sharing that will teach you how to take the first step toward your dreams.
