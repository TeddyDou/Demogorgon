ALUMNI RECOGNITION AWARDS
Every year the Concordia University Alumni Association honours valuable contributions made by alumni, students, friends, faculty and staff.
Meet the 2017 Alumni Recogition Awards recipients
Listen to the 2017 banquet podcast
Recipients
Ghislain Arsenault Jr.
Humberto Santos Award of Merit
This most prestigious honour is awarded to an alumnus or alumna who has made a lifetime contribution of exceptional leadership and service to the Concordia and to the community.
Sandra Paikowsky
Alumna of the Year
Awarded to an alumna or alumnus who has demonstrated professional excellence and community leadership. 
Paul Desbaillets
Alumnus of the Year
Awarded to an alumna or alumnus who has demonstrated professional excellence and community leadership. 
Robert Frances
Benoît Pelland Distinguished Service Award
Awarded to an alumnus or alumna who has demonstrated long-term commitment to outstanding service to the association and university.
Michel Gendron
Outstanding Staff Award
Awarded to a Concordia University staff member who has made an exceptional contribution to the alumni association or to the university community.
Philippe Caignon
Alumni Award for Excellence in Teaching
Awarded to a member of Concordia’s teaching staff who has shown superior knowledge, teaching ability and accessibility to students. 
Julia Sutera Sardo
Outstanding Student Award
Awarded to a Concordia student who has demonstrated qualities as an outstanding student leader and contributor to student life.
Sudhir Jha
Young Alumnus of the Year Award
Awarded to a graduate of the last 10 years who has demonstrated professional excellence and community leadership.
