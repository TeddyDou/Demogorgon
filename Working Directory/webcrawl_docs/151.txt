The Arts and Science Federation of Associations is the largest faculty association of Concordia University, representing over 20,000 undergraduate Arts and Science students.
ASFA is composed by 7 Executives elected every academic year, 33 Member Association Councillors
and 5 Independent Councillors.
 
Concordia University is located on Kanien’kehá:ka (Mohwak) traditional territory.
