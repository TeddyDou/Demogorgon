Graduate Curriculum Committee (GCC)
Role
Graduate Curriculum Revisions begin at the Program/Department level.
Once approved by the Department Curriculum Committee, they are reviewed at Faculty and University levels following the Graduate Curriculum Approval Process.
Forms for submission of Course Change/New Course, or Program Change/New Program Description, can be found in the Provost website .
For new graduate program proposal guidelines, please contact  Frédérica Martin .
