The Art of Leadership
November 29, 2017, 8:30 a.m. – 5 p.m.
The Art of Leadership brings together the most respected names in thought leadership in a place of inspirational interaction. Surrounded by a network of like-minded individuals, we design unique conferences that are built around the stories, ideas, discoveries and collaborative moments that drive achievement.
Former vice-president Joe Biden, the 47th vice-president of the United States will be the headline speaker at The Art of Leadership conferences in Montreal on November 29, 2017. 
Vice-president Biden will be joined by Amanda Lang (Bloomberg TV Canada anchor, business journalist and bestselling author), Welby Altidor (author & Executive Creative Director formerly with Cirque du Soleil), and New York Times bestselling authors, Dr. Tasha Eurich and Vince Molinaro.
Please register online  to reserve your place! Faculty, staff and other members of the Concordia community can take advantage of a special $399 promotional rate. 
A special promotional rate of $199 is also available for Concordia students – to learn more, please consult the student registration info page .
