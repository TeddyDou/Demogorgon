Weekly Meditation
September 12, 2017 – November 28, 2017, 12:15 p.m. – 1:15 p.m.
Come and experience the refreshing practice of simply being present through sitting and walking meditation. The traditional technique known as Shamatha, or "peaceful abiding", will be introduced by an experienced instructor.
A brief instruction will be given at the beginning of each session, followed by silent practice.
Everyone is welcome! This meditation practice will be offered weekly from September 12 - November 28. 
Where
Room Z 05, Z Annex (2090 Mackay), Sir George Williams Campus
Cost
This event is free
