Published on May 8, 2017
http://www.concordia.ca/planned-giving | In this video, Christine Lengvari, BSc 72,  president and CEO of Lengvari Financial Inc. — a boutique insurance firm in Montreal — explains why she gave a one million dollars to Concordia University.
The Christine Lengvari Endowment Fund will support scholarships for female students in the Kenneth Woods Portfolio Management Program at JMSB and will also promote research at Concordia’s PERFORM Centre.
