Stephen Brunt
Author, Sportsnet
STEPHEN BRUNT, Author for  Sportsnet  (anotha’ one), will be speaking at our conference in November!  #JMSM17
Stephen is an award-winning journalist from Sportsnet. After studying journalism at University of Western Ontario, he started as an intern at The Globe in 1982. He didn’t start his successful career in sports writing until 1985. He has over 30 years of experience of writing in the sports industry. He has written numerous books including the #1 Canadian Best Seller, Searching for Bobby Orr. Currently he is a columnist for sportsnet.ca and Sportsnet magazine.
Excited to have him here for our conference!
