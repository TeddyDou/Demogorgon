Club Description: 
The Concordia French Society is an association, enabling students, a majority of French speakers, to facilitate their arrival and integration in Montreal through various social and cultural events. Concordia French Society is looking forward to building a strong community, as well as a student network where everyone is curious, festive, open-minded and ambitious in life to socialize freely with others. La French Society consists of numerous annual events such as the famous Wine&Cheese, private parties (nightclub, rooftops), networking events, oenology classes with experts, cultural visits and trips around Canada. 
In the end, the goal is to make realize, all interested students, the energy and attractiveness of Montreal studies, valuable encounters and the overall quality of life.
