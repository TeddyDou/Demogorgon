Our Faculty
For 40 years , the Department of Journalism has trained journalists whose stories reach hundreds of thousands of readers and listeners across the world every day. All of our faculty members are former or currently working journalists. They bring real-world experience into the classrooms and are dedicated to producing professional journalists trained to ask important and critical questions about our world.
