Past Volumes
Since 2004, CUJAH’s has provided a platform to showcase the talent and achievements of Fine Arts students at Concordia University. Supervised by faculty advisers, the journal annually publishes ten to fifteen outstanding essays written by undergraduate students enrolled in Concordia’s Department of Art History. Here you can read a full text version of all past volumes and featured essays.
