Reggies Solidarity Cooperative
After a recent strong reopening, Concordia’s official student bar is now entering its first official academic year of operating. Along with a new modern look, Reggies now offers a wide selection of quality food and beverages at an affordable price. Whether it is an after-class hangout, meeting or a place to study, Reggies is the hotspot on campus with plenty of entertainment and a wide range of activities.
In addition to being a social hub for events organized by Concordia’s student groups and faculty, its friendly staff is always ready to serve you to the fullest. And, now as a solidarity cooperative, it is time to give back more to its patrons. So, what are you waiting for? Become a member today!
