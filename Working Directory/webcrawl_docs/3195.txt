Club Description: 
The Concordia Polish Students' Association, hereinafter referred to as (CPSA) is an organization led by a group of dynamic students dedicated in enhancing the student life at Concordia University. CPSA strives to unite the Polish community in Montreal, Quebec and as well promote to other students our rich culture by hosting an array of social, cultural and networking events. 
Everyone is welcome :)
