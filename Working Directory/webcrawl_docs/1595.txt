Office of the Chief Communications Officer
University Communications Services (UCS) manages Concordia’s communications initiatives. UCS is the university’s hub for public and media relations, marketing, branding, advertising, public affairs, and internal and digital communications.

Key services & units
UCS is structured as a matrix model made up of three interrelated units that collaborate closely on all communications activities.
Brand and marketing
Web Communications
Manages the university’s digital communications initiatives that include websites, social media, mobile, search engine optimization and mass email campaigns and newsletters
Director, Web Communications: TBD
 
Studio
Establishes and promotes Concordia’s visual identity through design and logos, and coordinates graphic design, photography and videography services
Brand and Studio Manager: Véronique Verthuy
 
Publications
Creates and distributes digital and print publications about and for the Concordia community
News Director: Heather Thompson
 
Recruitment Marketing
Develops innovative strategies to promote Concordia and attract top students
Manager, Student Recruitment Marketing and Communications:  Candace Jacobs
Advancement and alumni communications
Plans and executes fundraising campaigns and activities for alumni, students, donors and friends
Director, Communications: Sylvain-Jacques Desjardins
Communications and public affairs
Handles the university’s internal communications and media relations, as well as communications within Concordia’s four faculties, libraries, Co-operative education, Centre for Continuing Education and research and graduate studies.
Director, Public Relations:  Mary-Jo Barr
Director, Internal Communications: Karen McCarthy
