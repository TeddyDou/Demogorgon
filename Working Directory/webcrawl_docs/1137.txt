Career Management Services for employers
JMSB's Career Management Services (CMS) would like to work with you to develop a long-standing relationship that will enhance your recruitment objectives by targeting our top-notch business graduates.
At the Career Management Services (CMS) department of the John Molson School of Business (JMSB), we proactively provide our students and alumni with the skills and tools to confidently manage their talent. We also build sustainable relationships with our partners in the business community. CMS would like to work with you to develop a long-standing relationship that will enhance your recruitment objectives by targeting our top-notch business graduates. Read more of our  employer testimonials  to get a sense of of what we can offer you.
