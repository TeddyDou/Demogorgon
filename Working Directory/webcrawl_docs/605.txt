Parking on Loyola Campus
Daily outdoor parking lots
Parking Lots and Rates
Terrebonne Parking lot (P5)
Jesuit Parking Lot (P2)
Sports Complex Parking Lot (P7)
$ 6.00 /flat fee
Find on Map
Student Parking Permits (per term)
Student Parking permits are available for the Sports Complex Parking Lot ONLY
To register, go to myconcordia.ca / Student Services / Student Parking on the first day of class (January, May, and September)
$ 230 per term
Find on Map
Monthly parking permits (Faculty & Staff Only)
Monthly Parking Permit Rates
Monthly Parking Permits are available for the following spaces:
AD Building : $115/month
Hingston Hall : $115/month
Sports Complex : $115/month
Find on Map
1. A parking agreement must be signed and respected when owning a parking permit.
2. Monthly payments can be made through Automatic Payroll Deduction.
To request information on parking permits, locations and availability, email parking@concordia.ca .
To Register for a parking permit, go to myconcordia - Auxiliary Services .
 
Rates include GST and PST.  Rates are subject to change.
