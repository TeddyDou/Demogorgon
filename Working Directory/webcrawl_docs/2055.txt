Nadia Naffi speaks at the 14th Annual Pierre Elliot Trudeau Foundation Forum
Posted on November 16, 2017
|
Nadia Naffi , full-time faculty in the Education Department, PhD candidate in Educational Technology and public scholar shared her research findings with a very engaged audiance at the 14th Annual Pierrre Elliot Trudeau Foundation Forum .
Naffi was invited to participate in the "Democraty, Journalism, and Citizenship: What Girls Have to Say" pannel, chaired by professor Mélanie Milette, a Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation scholar in 2011 and a professor in the department of social and public communication at UQAM, as well as a member of the Laboratoire de communication médiatisée par ordinateur(LabCMO, UQAM and Université Laval) and TheFourchettes – Critical Methods in Technoculture collective.
