JMAS Tax Clinic 2017
On the weekend of March 25th and 26th, Concordia’s John Molson Accounting Society (JMAS),
a University student-run organization, will be preparing income tax returns FREE of charge for Québec residents.
About the Clinic
During the weekend of March 25-26th, 2017,  Concordia’s John Molson Accounting Society (JMAS) will be preparing income tax returns FREE OF CHARGE for Quebec residents.
John Molson School of Business
MB Building, Lobby/Entrance Floor
1450 Rue Guy, Montreal, H3H 0A1
Doors Open: at 10:00 a.m.
Doors Close: at 5:00 p.m.
Participants will be served on a FIRST COME FIRST SERVE basis
Criteria
Income:
Individual: Maximum income of $25,000 or less
Couples: Combined income of $30,000 or less
Individual with a dependant: $30,000 or less
Additional $2,000 in income per dependent
Total interest and investment income < $1,000 or less
***Must be a resident of Quebec***
Those who do not meet the above requirements are NOT eligible for the tax clinic.
People ineligible for the tax clinic:
Deceased
Bankruptcy
Capital gains or Losses
Self-Employment Income
Rental Income
Foreign Income
What to Bring
Personal Documents and required information:
Social Insurance Number (SIN)
Residency status for tax purposes. 
Consult CRA website for more information : CRA Website – Status
Your Notice of Assessment 2015 (If possible)
All official slips that will be sent to you by a Ministry or Government body, your employer, your financial institution, your school, or others.

All receipts related to possible Tax Credits
For more information, click on the Credits tab
