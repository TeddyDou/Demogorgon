Department of Applied Human Sciences
AHSC at Concordia offers a range of challenging and innovative programs in human relations, leisure sciences, therapeutic recreation, community service, family life education, human systems intervention, and youth work.
The educational philosophy of our department centers on experience-based learning or learning-by-doing . Most of our programs include fieldwork or internships outside the university.
