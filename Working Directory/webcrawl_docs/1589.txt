Lockers
Locker rentals made easy!
Lockers are available for rental in buildings across both campuses. Its real simple to rent a locker online and you can select the campus, building, floor and desired location of the locker that best accommodates you.
How to rent a locker
1. Go to myconcordia.ca
2. Click on: student services
3. Select: Locker Services
Combination locks are provided by the University for all lockers.
The combination number will be provided to you once you've registered for a locker via the Portal.
Locker Fees
Locker fees:
Fall/Winter Session:
$25.00 Tall/Custom lockers
$15.00  Half size standard lockers
Summer Session:
$12.50 Tall/Custom lockers
$10.00  Half size standard lockers
The fee is automatically charged to your student account.
If you need a receipt for this, you will have to request one from Student Accounts where the charge is applied.
The fee must be paid within 30 days to avoid incurring interest on your account.
Payment can be made using the same method in which you pay your tuition.
 
Using a combination lock or if you forget your combination number
If you've never used a combination lock, see easy instruction below on how to open one:
First, You will receive a combination number which opens the lock. Let's say your combination number is: 25-11-33
Firmly push the handles all the way into the base and follow the steps:
Turn three whole turns to the right (–>) and stop at the first number (25)
Turn one whole turn to the left (<–), passing your first number, and stop at the second number (11)
Turn right (–>) and stop at the last number (33)
You're done. Follow the same for the combination numbers you are given and your lock should open!
You can also check out the video on how to open a combination loc k
If you've followed the instructions carefully, and your lock still won't open
Email lockers@concordia.ca and we'll send someone over to verify and/or change the lock.
If we change the lock, we will put the new combination number on the Portal, in your locker data.
If you forget your combination number, please note that combination numbers will NOT be given over the phone or email.
1. You can log on to your locker Portal and select: View my locker data.
or, if you cannot access your data...
2. You (and only if you are the person who registered for the locker) must present yourself at the Locker service office, in person with ID, during regular business hours. You cannot send someone else on your behalf.
 
 
Important Dates: Fall & Winter Term
Rentals are valid from September (first day of class) to April 30th
Lockers not being renewed for the summer session MUST be emptied by April 30th
Contents left in lockers after April 30th will be discarded if you have not renewed your locker via the Portal
 
How do I renew my locker for the Summer Term
you must renew between April 3 - 15
Go to MyConcordia portal, click on "view my locker data or renew my locker rental", then "view record".  
The renewal link is located below the combination number.
If you missed the renewal period above:
You must empty the locker contents by April 30 otherwise all content will be discarded.
You can register for a new locker the second week of May.
The locker fee for renewal remains the same as locker fee for that session.
The renewal option is a benefit to keep the same locker into the next session and avoid having to empty the contents and move into another locker.
Important Dates: Summer Term
Rentals are valid from May 15 to August 31st
Lockers not renewed for the fall term MUST be emptied by August 31st.
Contents left in lockers after August 31st will be discarded if you have not renewed your locker via the Portal
How do I renew my locker for the Fall/Winter term
you must renew between August 1 - 20
Go to MyConcordia portal, click on "view my locker data or renew my locker rental", then "view record".  
The renewal link is located below the combination number.
If you missed the renewal period above:
You must empty the locker contents by August 31st otherwise all content will be discarded.
You can register for a new locker on the first day of class in September.
The locker fee for renewal remains the same as locker fee for that session.
The renewal option is a benefit to keep the same locker into the next session and avoid having to empty the contents and move into another locker.
Important notice
All items left behind in the locker will be immediately disposed of following the deadline date - unless the locker is renewed.
It is the responsibility of the individual who rented a locker to remove all content within the locker by the term deadline dates mentioned above.
A locker cannot be renewed after the renewal period. Students will have to remove contents (to not have them discarded) by rental end date, and can return to rent a locker during the rental period.
Concordia University is not responsible for the replacement of any disposed contents which remained in the lockers past the removal due date.
Concordia University is not responsible for any loss, theft or damage to contents stored in lockers on University premises.
Cancellation Policy: A full refund applies if you cancel your locker up to 30 days from the rental date - otherwise there is no refund for cancellations.
The combination locks are property of Concordia University. All locks must remain on the lockers when your rental period has expired otherwise a $10 fee will be applied to your student account.
FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
I forgot my locker combination
Locker combinations will NOT be given over the phone or email.
You can log on to your locker Portal and select: View my locker data. 
If you cannot access your data; You (and only if you are the person who registered for the locker) must present yourself at the Locker service office, in person with ID, during regular business hours. You cannot send someone else on your behalf.
I cannot open my lock
Follow these instructions: let's say your combination number is: 25-11-33
Firmly push the handles all the way into the base and follow the steps:
a) Turn three whole turns to the right (–>) and stop at the first number (25)
b) Turn one whole turn to the left (<–), passing your first number, and stop at the second number (11)
c) Turn right (–>) and stop at the last number (33)
You're done. Follow the same for the combination numbers you are given and your lock should open!
You can also check out the video on how to open a combination lock .
If you have followed all the above steps and still cannot open the lock, please send an email to lockers@concordia.ca
I missed the deadline to remove my locker content and items are gone and/or the lock is changed.
As per the terms and conditions - to which you agreed when renting your locker -  all items which remain in the lockers past the end date of the locker rental session, are immediately discarded. Once the locker is cleaned out, a new lock is placed on the locker for the next rental session.
How can I cancel my locker?
Send an email to lockers@concordia.ca and include: your student ID number, locker number and building floor, with the subject title: Cancel My Locker.
You must empty out all contents from the locker immediately once you have requested your cancellation, otherwise items left behind will be discarded.
If your locker is cancelled before 30 days from the rental date, a full refund will apply. No refunds are made after 30 days from the rental period. Lockers will send you an email confirming that your locker has been cancelled.
If I have a locker for Fall & Winter, can I renew it for the next Fall & Winter session without renting it for the Summer Session?
You can renew the same locker for the next session only, if not the content in the locker must be removed by the end date of your rental otherwise the items will be immediately discarded.
You can always try to rent the same one the following year, provided it has not already been rented.
My lock no longer works, even though I was able to open it previously.
Send an email to lockers@concordia.ca and include: your student ID number, locker number and building floor where the locker is located. We will send someone to verify and change lock as needed. Lockers Services will send you an email advising the lock has been changed and your new combination will appear in the Portal for you to view your locker data.
How can I change my locker?
1. Rent a new locker: You can rent a new locker by going back to the Portal just as you rented the original locker, and select a new locker, location, as desired.
2. Cancel the original: Send an email to lockers@concordia.ca and include: your student ID number, locker number and building floor, with the subject title: Replace My Locker.
You must move out all contents to your new locker immediately once you have requested your cancellation, otherwise items left behind will be discarded.
Lockers Services will send you an email confirming that your original locker has been cancelled and now replaced with the new locker rented.
