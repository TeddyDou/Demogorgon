Service hours
Sir George Williams Campus
Our offices are open from Monday to Friday, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Our walk-in clinic hours to meet with a financial aid advisor are every afternoon from 1 p.m. to 4 p.m.

Our office is open every Tuesday from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. and 1 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.
