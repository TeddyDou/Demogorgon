We promote a positive university experience. We support a sustainable Concordia.     We defend student rights.We serve students.
The CSU represents over 35,000 undergraduate students and is run by students for students. We actively work to build a better Concordia by offering essential services, throwing great events, and defending students rights within the university and beyond.
