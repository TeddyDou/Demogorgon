Robert Weladji's Explore Concordia page
Education
PhD (Norwegian University of Life Sciences)
Research interests
Behavioural ecology of large herbivores, life  history strategies and  evolutionary ecology, population ecology, population dynamics, terrestrial ecosystems ecology, wildlife  conservation and management, wildlife ecology, welfare of captive wildlife.
Teaching activities
Population Ecology
Biodiversity & Ecology

N. A. Melnycky*, R.B. Weladji, Øystein Holand & Mauri Nieminen (2013) Scaling of antler size in reindeer (Rangifer tarandus): sexual dimorphism and variability in resource allocation. Journal of Mammalogy 94:1371-1379.
