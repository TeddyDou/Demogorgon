PROFESSIONAL PROGRAMS
There are a number of graduate course-based program options available. You can study engineering with an industry-driven curriculum and co-op options, or you can pursue an MBA degree at our John Molson School of Business, rated second best in Canada by The Economist  and called the second-greenest MBA in the world by Corporate Knights.
We offer professional programs in all faculties
