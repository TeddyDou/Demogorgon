Meredith Geisler
Meredith Geisler
Senior Vice-President of Communications, Tandem Sports & Entertainment
Meredith is responsible for the public relations of many big name athletes including Jeremy Lin, Tim Duncan, and Ray Allen. She has over 25 years of experience in PR and has worked for companies such as Fila USA and Advantage International.
 
She also is a professor at American University, Georgetown University, and George Washington University.
