Undertake an integrative, interdisciplinary education
Delve into some of the most complex and challenging issues facing humankind at this honours college. Examine the arts and sciences from global, multicultural perspectives. We offer a Minor in Sustainability Studies and Minor in Diversity and the Contemporary World, as well as a membership program Read more about us .
