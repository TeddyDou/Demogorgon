A centre of excellence for aerospace-related research and education
CIADI is a coordinated umbrella platform that promotes and supports aerospace research and education at Concordia.
How to apply Contact us
CIADI provides leading-edge know-how among engineering students engaged in aerospace design and innovation and supports the development and management of multipartner aerospace research projects.
