Club Description: 
The Upstarters Club provides students with the opportunity to get in contact with entrepreneurs to learn how companies are born, how to think as entrepreneurs in their daily lives and even how to apply this knowledge to their careers. All  while connecting with like minded students from other faculties to expand their academic and professional horizons. The university environment offers tons of great resources for developing new ideas and Upstarters' main goals are to bring valuable events for students of all background that are interested in entrepreneurship.
