Health & wellness
Health and wellness are essential to student success and Concordia offers many professional student services that put your needs first.
Counselling
Stressed, anxious, relationship problems, in the wrong major? If these or any other issues are affecting your life or your studies, then counselling can help.
Counselling & Psychological Services provides students with access to professional licensed psychologists, psychotherapists and counsellors will collaborate with you to achieve psychological health, wellness and student success.
Health Services also offers  mental health-related counselling and assessment services , as does the Access Centre for Students with Disabilities
All of these services are overseen by the Office of Campus Wellness and Support .
Health Services
Health Services offers Concordia students an on-campus wellness clinic and health promotion center. We offer: 
Appointments  with nurses and doctors
Urgent care clinic
Tests , including sexually transmitted infections testing
Vaccines
Reliable  patient resources  and  healthy living  information
Learn more about using the service and find answers to Frequently Asked Questions .
Visit health services
Sexual assault resources
The Sexual Assault Resource Centre provides confidential and non-judgmental support to Concordia University students, staff and faculty of all genders and orientations who have been affected by sexual violence and/or harassment. We are committed to providing services that are inclusive, appropriate and relevant to a diversity of people and survivor experiences.

Spiritual life
Concordia addresses the spiritual needs of its students through the Multi-faith and Spirituality Centre, which provides a community hub for students, resources for faith and spiritual practice and spaces to study or relax. You can find the following services to maintain your spiritual health: 
Spiritual guidance
Faith on campus
Events and activities
Prayer and recreational spaces to book
Student emergency and food fund
