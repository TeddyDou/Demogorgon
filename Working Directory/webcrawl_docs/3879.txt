Patrick Boivin
President & CEO, Montreal Alouettes
Patrick Boivin started his career in sports working for the Montreal Canadiens. There he spent over over 10 years working in several different positions. Most notably, Patrick Boivin held the position of Director of Hockey Operations.
After his time spent at the Canadiens, he went on to be the Director of Recreation and Athletics at Concordia University. He was an influential part of making the Concordia Stingers who they are today. During his time with Concordia, he helped enable the teams to be more competitive and improved the facilities on campus.
In December of 2016, Boivin left Concordia to become the President and CEO of the Montreal Alouettes. In this position, he leads the organization’s management, and the team’s and business’ development.
