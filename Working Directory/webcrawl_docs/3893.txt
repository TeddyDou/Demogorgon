Dimitri Filipovic
Author, Sportsnet
DIMITRI FILIPOVIC, also a  Sportsnet  Author, will be joining us at the  22nd Annual John Molson Sports Business Conference !
After graduating from the  Simon Fraser University , Dimitri went on to write for Rogers Sportsnet. He is a prominent figure on Twitter and well admired for his Podcast “The Hockey PDOcast” which tangles in the world of hockey analytics.
After writing for several websites including the Canucks Army, in March 2015 he was hired is a freelance Hockey Analyst for Sportsnet. In March 2016 he officially became a full time member of the Sportsnet and he has not looked back ever since.
We look forward to having him here!
