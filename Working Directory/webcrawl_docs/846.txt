Snacks n' Stories: An Interfaith Holiday Potluck
Come unwind before finals with fellow students over some tasty snacks (bonus points if they’re holiday specialties!) and stories. Snacks n’ Stories is a casual evening to celebrate celebrations, get to know each other and an excuse to take a break!   Do you have a favourite holiday memory? Do you have a funny Hanukkah, Diwali or Ramadan tale? No matter the religious holiday, come share your stories! Bring a vegetarian item* (if you can) or just bring your best stories.
When
November 21, 2017, 5:30 p.m. – 8:30 p.m.
Where
Sir George Williams Campus Z - Z Annex Z 105/106 (2090 Mackay)

Concordia Multi-faith Day of Action
Join us for our monthly Day of Action at the MFSC. We’ll be making cookies again (they were too much of a hit not to!) along with sandwiches, then heading out to feed the needy.   This time we’ll also be thinking about food and spirituality. What do the different spiritual traditions have to say about feeding others and the needy? How do they express it? Come learn, get inspired and share your own wisdom and thoughts!

November 28, 2017, 10:30 a.m. – 1:30 p.m.
