Club Description: 
The Concordia Italian Association represents students of Italian and Italian-Canadian origin, equivalent to approximately 10 percent of the student body at Concordia University. We wish to promote Italian culture through a variety of events and activities both on campus and in the community at large. The CIA welcomes all students who have an appreciation for the Italian heritage to join.
