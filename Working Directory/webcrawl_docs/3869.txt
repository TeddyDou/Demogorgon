Jordan Nashen
Director of Marketing & Player Relations, Roy Sports Group
JORDAN NASHEN, Director of Marketing & Player Relations for Roy Sports Group, will be speaking at the  22nd Annual John Molson Sports Business Conference !
As the director of marketing and player relations, Jordan Nashen is responsible for marketing and endorsement initiatives for all of the RSG Hockey clients. Before working for RSG Hockey, Nashen worked for the Tampa Bay Lightning, the Charlotte Hornets, and CAA Hockey. He has not only worked with sports clients but also some entertainers and comedians at Hollywood’s top firms.
Nashen was born and raised here in Montreal and graduated from JMSB in 2009. We’re so excited to have this JMSB alumni back at our conference this year!
