Co-operative Education Information Session
November 24, 2017, 9:30 a.m. – 11 a.m.
Are you interested in experiential learning that combines study with work? Get career work experience, apply what you are learning in the real world, work full-time, earn academic credits all the while earning a salary. Find out what academic programs are available at Concordia University as a co-op option.
This session is jointly given by the Institute of Cooperative Education and International Students Office and provides information about benefits of co-op education and proper immigration documents for international students who wish to pursue it.
Who: First- and second-year international undergraduate and first-year graduate students

Where
