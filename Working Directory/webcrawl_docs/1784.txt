Joel Dunham
Postdoctoral Researcher and Software Developer
Interests
Software development for linguistic fieldwork, endangered languages fieldwork, finite state morphology, formal semantics
I am interested in facilitating linguistic fieldwork by creating software that is useful to linguists, educators, and learners. My research interests include endangered languages fieldwork, finite-state morphological parsers, and the semantics of tense and aspect constructions.
