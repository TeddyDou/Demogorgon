GUIDANCE SERVICE
Do you have academic or University-related questions? As part of our Academic Guidance service, the JMAC team is here to help! Whether it is providing you with a solution or directing you to the appropriate resources, we are here to support you in all of your academic endeavors!
We can address a multitude of topics such as:
Advice about time management & stress management
Deciding which classes to take for your major or minor
Effective study strategies
How to get involved in student life
How to search for an internships
Exposing you to valuable resources that exist within the University
and much more!
There are many ways to get into contact us during our office hours. Drop in to our office S1.447, send us a message on Facebook, e-mail info@jmacjmsb.ca, or call us at 514-848-2424 ext. 2800!
