Club Description: 
Liberal Concordia represents students at Concordia University who are members of the Young Liberals of Canada (YLC) and as well as those who are interested in becoming part of or being involved in activities regarding the Liberal Party, in its capacity as an official student organization.
All news and information on upcoming events will be publicly posted on our Facebook page: www.facebook.com/liberalconcordia
Any questions or comments? Share them with us!
Contact and follow us on Twitter (@LibConcordia) or like our page on Facebook!
